Tips to Overcome Monotony

Morning Hacks for NYUAD Students

  • ★

    Start Your Day with Movement

    Starting your day with some physical activity can boost your mood and energy levels. Whether it's a jog around campus or stretching exercises, a little movement can help you feel more awake and focused.
  • ★

    Change Up Your Routine

    Breaking away from a rigid routine can provide a refreshing change. Even small adjustments, like switching up your morning activities or taking a different route to class, can inject excitement into your day.
  • ★

    Morning Journaling

    Journaling in the morning can clear your mind and set a positive intention for the day. It helps you reflect, express your emotions, and clarify your goals, ensuring a productive and mindful start to your day.
  • ★

    Plan Something Fun for After Class

    Having something to look forward to after class, like a social meetup or a personal activity, can create anticipation and break the cycle of routine. It helps you stay motivated and make the most of your time at NYUAD.
  • ★

    Listen to a Podcast or Playlist

    Listening to a podcast or playlist in the morning can provide inspiration, learning, or entertainment, depending on your mood. Choose something that stimulates your mind or boosts your energy as you start your day.
