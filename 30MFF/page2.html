Tips to Overcome Monotony

Morning Hacks for NYUAD Students

  1. Start Your Day with Movement - Whether it's a quick jog around campus or a short yoga session, getting your body moving in the morning increases energy levels and helps clear your mind.
  2. Change Up Your Routine - Try altering your morning routine. If you usually go to the gym, take a walk instead. A small change can make your day feel more interesting and break the monotony.
  3. Morning Journaling - Spend 10 minutes writing about how you feel, your goals for the day, or even some gratitude thoughts. This will help set a positive tone for the day and reduce any feelings of monotony.
  4. Plan Something Fun for After Class - Having something to look forward to, whether it's meeting a friend for coffee or attending a club event, can break up the monotony of your daily routine.
  5. Listen to a Podcast or Playlist - Engage your mind with something new during your commute or while having breakfast. Whether it's an educational podcast or an energizing playlist, it can shift your mood and give you fresh insights to start the day.
